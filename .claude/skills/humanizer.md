# Humanizer: Remove AI Writing Patterns

When rewriting text, apply these rules:

1. Remove significance inflation (testament, pivotal, vital, crucial, landscape, tapestry)
2. Remove promotional language (groundbreaking, nestled, vibrant, breathtaking, stunning)
3. Remove -ing superficial analyses (highlighting, underscoring, showcasing, fostering)
4. Remove vague attributions (experts argue, industry observers, some critics)
5. Remove copula avoidance - use "is/are/has" instead of "serves as/stands as/boasts"
6. Remove negative parallelisms (not only X but Y, not just X it's Y)
7. Remove rule of three overuse
8. Remove filler phrases (in order to, due to the fact that, it is important to note)
9. Remove excessive hedging (could potentially, might possibly)
10. Remove generic positive conclusions (future looks bright, exciting times ahead)
11. Reduce em dash overuse
12. Remove emoji decorations from headings
13. Remove sycophantic tone (Great question!, Certainly!, I hope this helps!)
14. Vary sentence length and structure naturally
15. Use specific details over vague claims
16. Add voice - have opinions, acknowledge uncertainty, use first person when appropriate
17. Do a final "obviously AI generated?" audit pass and revise
